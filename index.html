How to Train

Listen to or read each premise, then internally generate semantic content that obeys every stated relation. Respond MATCH when the current trial has the same scored relational structure as the trial N positions earlier—not merely similar words or symbols.

Mode 6 — Recursive Invention

Two directional operators are composed and bound to an invented symbol. That invention can return as an operand, forcing reconstruction and further transformation of the prior semantic object.

Mode 7 — Ontological Parallax

The prompt adds an ontological category and a precise Inner, Outer and Archetypal perspective order. Its objective signature combines the category's mirrored family, the perspective permutation and the composed directional topology.

Inner Division through V … Outer Division through B … So Archetypal Division becomes invention Z.

A structural match may use different letters, directions or a mirrored category such as Multiplication, provided the full hidden signature remains isomorphic.

Accessibility guarantee

The program constructs one final premise string, writes that exact string to the live visual premise region, and sends that same string to browser speech synthesis. The visible and spoken channels therefore cannot silently diverge.
