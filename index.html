How to Train

Listen to or read each premise, then internally generate one structure satisfying every node, direction, ontology and perspective. Respond MATCH only when the current trial has the same complete scored structure as the trial N positions earlier.

Compact auditory grammar

Every relation uses the same order: category-bound node; direction to category-bound node. Example: “Inner Division U; east to Outer Multiplication M.” Semicolons mark auditory boundaries. “Of”, “connects”, explanatory definitions and the spoken word “Archetypal” are excluded.

A bare category name is the unmarked or archetypal form. Therefore “Connection M” is distinct from “Inner Connection M” and “Outer Connection M”, without saying “Archetypal Connection”. Categories belong to nodes; direction words never masquerade as ontological operators.

The ontological field

The nine categories are All, Difference, Action, Division, Connection, Multiplication, Projection, Encompassment and Completion. Complementary mirrors are All–Completion, Difference–Encompassment, Action–Projection and Division–Multiplication; Connection mirrors itself.

Inner and Outer meanings are learned operators, not definitions repeated inside every trial. Category composition uses an explicit nine-position computational field. This is a game formalisation for deterministic scoring, not a claim that the source theory supplied this exact composition table.

Cognitive interference

The slider changes how the logic of each new premise competes with the complete logic of premises already held in the N-back window. It does not create difficulty by recycling arbitrary letters.

Higher settings preferentially generate non-matches that preserve most of the target’s ontology, perspective and topology while changing one decisive relation; reinstate the structure of an intervening premise; permute the same operators across different roles; dissociate construction from computed outcome; or, in recursive modes, preserve visible wrappers while changing inherited lineage. Symbols are refreshed after the logical candidate is chosen. An invention symbol is retained only when it genuinely names inherited recursive structure.

Mode 1 — Cardinal Streaming

Bind two independently categorised nodes through one cardinal relation. Example: “Inner Division U; east to Outer Multiplication M.”

Mode 2 — Relational Reversal

Hold the relation and its reversal together. “Reverse” swaps the nodes, reverses direction, mirrors each category and exchanges Inner with Outer; the unmarked form remains unmarked.

Mode 3 — Triadic Composition

Integrate three node ontologies and two linked directions. The resulting ontology and form are computed from the full triad and its turn topology.

Mode 4 — Topological Equivalence

Integrate two complete dyads as relations-between-relations. The second dyad is the rotated ontological mirror of the first; either dyad may appear first without changing the scored equivalence.

Mode 5 — Operator Composition

Integrate four category-bound nodes and three linked directions as one quaternary structure. The final ontology is computed from all nodes and both directional turns.

Mode 6 — Recursive Invention

Bind two nodes, compute their result and name it as an invention. A later “invention” node reopens its stored ontology, form, topology, lineage and recursion depth beneath the newly spoken wrapper category.

Mode 7 — Ontological Parallax

Coordinate Inner, Outer and unmarked nodes in one triad; compute both node synthesis and the categories of its two relations; reify their relation as an invention; then hold its mirrored reversal.

What MATCH means

Scoring uses ontologies, forms, node roles, directional topology, computed results and inherited invention digests. Higher modes permit only their specified structural invariances, such as global rotation, reversal or dyad exchange. Repeated words alone cannot produce a match.

Accessibility guarantee

The program constructs one final premise string, writes that exact string to the live visual region and sends the same string to speech synthesis. The response window opens only after speech finishes; audio-only display, pause/resume replay, speech rate, volume and speech/background separation remain available.

This is a theoretically motivated relational-integration design. It is not validated evidence that training will increase general fluid intelligence.
